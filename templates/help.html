Frequently Asked Questions

How do feature votes work?

Voting allows our product team to gauge community interest. Features with the most votes get bumped up the queue during our review cycles. You can vote for a feature once per device.

Can I edit my feature request?

Currently, feature ideas cannot be edited once submitted. If you'd like to provide more details, feel free to submit a new feature request or send us an email with the request ID.
